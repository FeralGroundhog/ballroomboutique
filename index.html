Donatas Nacajus

Born in Lithuania, Donatas has been dancing since he was seven years old and has not stopped since. In his youth, he competed and won numberous dance competitions around the world, including the National Dance Sport Championship (twice). In 1998, he represented the Lithuanian National Dance Team's World Youth Division Dance Sport Championship held in Russia.

Donatas is a graduate of the Academy of Physical Education in the fields of Ballroom & Latin Dance as well as Physical Education. He was taught in London by Michael and Vicky Barrs.
